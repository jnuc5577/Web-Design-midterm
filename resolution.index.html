Resolution

On their way through the underworld and to Hades, grover and Percy find that the master bolt was hidden inside the sheild that Ares gave to them. They come to the realization that it was Ares who had stolen the master bolt all along. They make a deal with Hades to trade Hades' helm for the release of Percy's mother. Percy fights Ares after returning from the underworld. Percy wins the fight and Ares hands over the helm to Percy but proclaims that Percy has now made an enemy for life.

Percy returns the bolt to Zeus and returns the helm to Hades. Percy then finds that Hades has returned his mother and they are all safe and sound. Although, back at camp, Percy finds out that Luke, a former ally of the group had helped Ares with stealing the master bolt. The two fight and eventually Luke escapes and the show ends, leading into the next season/chapter.
